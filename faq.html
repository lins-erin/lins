Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Message us on WhatsApp and we'll reply instantly.

What sizes are available for T-shirts? +
We stock sizes from S to 3XL. If you need a specific size, just mention it when you contact us and we'll confirm availability right away.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept MTN Mobile Money, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money, and bank transfers. Cash on pickup is also available when you collect on campus.
Do you deliver outside Campus? +
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Ghana. Delivery charges may vary by location — contact us for your area's rate and we'll sort it out for you.
How long does delivery take? +
Campus deliveries typically take 1–2 business days. Nationwide deliveries take 2–5 business days depending on your region within Ghana.
Can I exchange or return an item? +
Yes, we accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery if the item is unused and in its original condition. Contact us on WhatsApp to arrange it — simple and stress-free.
Do you restock sold-out items? +
Yes! Follow us on Facebook or contact us on WhatsApp to be notified when your favourite items are back in stock.

The Process

How to Order

1

🛍 Browse & Choose

Head to our Shop page and pick the item(s) you want. Browse by category — Clothing, Footwear, or Perfumes — and click any product to view sizes, colours, and price.

2

💬 Contact Us

Reach us on WhatsApp, call, or email with your chosen item, size, and colour. You can also tap the Order on WhatsApp button directly on the product — it fills in the details automatically!

3

✅ Confirm & Pay

We confirm stock availability and the final price. You then make payment via Mobile Money, bank transfer, or cash on pickup — whichever works best for you.

4

🚀 Fast Delivery

We deliver directly to your location on or off campus. Campus orders arrive in 1–2 days, nationwide in 2–5 business days. Or arrange a quick pickup — your choice!
