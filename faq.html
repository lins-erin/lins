Common Questions

Frequently Asked

What sizes are available for T-shirts?+
We stock sizes from S to 3XL. Mention your size when you contact us and we'll confirm availability.
What payment methods do you accept?+
MTN Mobile Money, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money, bank transfers, and cash on pickup.
Do you deliver outside Campus?+
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Ghana. Contact us for your area's delivery rate.
How long does delivery take?+
Campus: 1–2 business days. Nationwide: 2–5 business days depending on your region.
Can I exchange or return an item?+
Yes! Exchanges within 7 days of delivery, if unused and in original condition. WhatsApp us to arrange.
Do you restock sold-out items?+
Yes! Follow us on Facebook or WhatsApp us to get notified when items are back in stock.
