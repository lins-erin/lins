About LIN's

The story behind Cape Coast's favourite campus fashion brand

Our Story

Born on Campus

LIN's began with a simple idea — that students and young professionals at the University of Cape Coast deserved access to authentic, stylish fashion without leaving campus or breaking the bank.

From plain T-shirts to premium sneakers, men's wear to luxury perfumes, every product we carry is handpicked for quality and style. We don't just sell clothes — we help you express who you are.

Today, LIN's delivers nationwide across Ghana while remaining rooted in the UCC campus community we love.

Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before placing your order.

What sizes are available for T-shirts?
We stock sizes from S to 3XL. If you need a specific size, just mention it when you contact us and we'll confirm availability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept MTN Mobile Money, Telecel Cash, AirtelTigo Money, and bank transfers. Cash on pickup is also available.
Do you deliver outside Campus?
Yes! We deliver nationwide across Ghana. Delivery charges may vary by location — contact us for your area's rate.
How long does delivery take?
Campus deliveries typically take 1–2 business days. Nationwide deliveries take 2–5 business days depending on your region.
Can I exchange or return an item?
Yes, we accept exchanges within 7 days of delivery if the item is unused and in its original condition. Contact us to arrange it.
Do you restock sold-out items?
Yes! Follow us on Facebook or contact us on WhatsApp to be notified when your favourite items are back in stock.
